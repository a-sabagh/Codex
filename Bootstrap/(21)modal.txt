MODAL:
the modal structure have three part:
1.The "Trigger" part:
	data-toggle="modal"
	data-target="#madalID"
2.The "Modal" part:
	#modalID
	.modal => (focuse on modal)
	.fade => (make transition with fade effect for modal)
	.modal-dialog => (set modal in center of page by margin)
3.The "Modal content" part:
	.modal-content
	.modal-header
	.modal-title
	.modal-body
	.modal-footer
	.close
	[data-dismiss="modal"]
----------------------------------------------------------------
modal size
	.modal-dialog.modal-sm
	.modal-dialog.modal-lg
